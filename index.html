Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SHEIN review program

How much can I earn reviewing SHEIN products?
Most reviewers earn between $200-$500 per month, with opportunities to earn up to $750 for detailed reviews. Your earnings depend on the number and quality of reviews you submit.
Do I need to purchase SHEIN products to review?
Yes, this program requires you to purchase SHEIN products to review. You keep all products you purchase - we're paying you for your honest feedback on items you buy.
How quickly will I get paid for my reviews?
Payments are processed within 24-48 hours after your review is approved. You can choose to receive payment via PayPal or direct bank transfer.
Is there any cost to join?
No, joining our reviewer community is completely free. We never ask for payment or credit card information.
How many reviews do I need to do?
You can do as many or as few reviews as you like. There's no minimum requirement - you're in control of how much you earn.
